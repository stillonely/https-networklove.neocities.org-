About arcrive
“Arcrive” is my video editing persona/brand. I’ve been editing since 2015 and rebranded to the name in 2018. I always wanted to go by a name that wasn't attached to any fandom or media. It is a play on words, “archive” which I found quite perfect because as the years pass by I find myself looking back at my work like an art collection.
This is entirely for fun, for myself, and a way for me to show my appreciation for the things I am interested in. I like to say it’s a hobby I picked up for a creative outlet and it’s one of my favorite things ever. Over the years, I have grown a bit sensitive and protective when it comes to editing and my edits. Hence, why it's been a bit hard for me to pick it up and create. Though when I do, I always find joy in the activity. I am still learning and there are many times where I could do better, but I try my best not to be so hard on myself (but if you ask me, I don’t really like my stuff LOL.)
Anyways! That is just a small intro. If you ever happen to stumble upon (probs not) on one of my edits, I hope you enjoy it because even if it’s not 100% perfect or you find it a bit cool, I at least try to put my heart into everything I make. Hopefully you can see that above anything.
