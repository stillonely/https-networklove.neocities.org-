Welcome >3< Hello. Ola. Hey. The name's Noah! I am a 23 year old transmasc..guy, dude..from California! This website is a personal archive / information site for my own pleasure, but also for the (gay) people on the web who come upon my social media accounts and want to get to know me!
I have no idea what I'm doing! I have never learned how to code sites. I was too young to explore on Tumblr and especially too young to have a MySpace. (Can you tell I held onto to what my siblings have told me.) I did take a web design class in college which was very fun, very basic, and I totally don't remember much! I am currently in school for Design (because graphic design is my passion AND my girlfriend.) so I know my academic journey of coding won't end there~
I am a big fan of web design :3 especially when I get to stare at it and go "Oooo, what does this button do!" (ah~ I love pressing things.) When it comes to my own personal journey of messing around with web design, I can say that: Listography is pretty neat, I still technically use it to this day. When the early beginning of Carrd era came upon kpop twitter, I instantly fell in love and constantly made different sites and always looked at other carrds because I loved how people got creative with it. I know my way around Notion and Rentry (also txti at one point. RIP.) and all the good gen z stuffs. and the most recent ones I have explored are mmm and straw page! but what do these site builders have in common? simple building sites where you don't have to worry about coding! :D Well. I mean you can pay for Carrd (been there, done that…) to import embeds. Rentry (and txti.. F for respects..) is a simple, cute, markdown service which I always had fun messing around with when I wanted something simple and easy to read.
Nonetheless, none of these sites gave me the freedom and challenge that Neocities brings. I was so happy that one day I happened to be scrolling through Tumblr and I found some users promoting their Neocities websites. It opened a whole new world for me that I didn't know was right at my digital fingertips. I am truly living my high school dream of wanting to join coding classes but didn't have the balls to LOL. And I just never thought something like this existed so I'm very excited to get things going!
I have a lot of plans for this website and hopefully I can accomplish them :3 like I said at the start (which btw if you made it this far?? thank you??) I want this website to be all things me! I hope to make this my main site where people can get to know me. All in one place, no more switching back and forth between sites because I get bored and want something new. I want to be able to dump all my stuff here and get lost in my interests and thoughts while being aware that a stranger can pass by and just perceive me because well. I've never been one to be scared of what digital footprints I leave behind so why not just combine them all together? ^3^ just an archive of me.
So nice to meet you, I hope you like it here.
You are safe. ⭐
